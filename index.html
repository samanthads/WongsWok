Wong's Wok

Located on Melville Ave just off of the Delmar Loop, Wong's Wok "gives cheap Chinese food a good name" according to the St. Louis Post-Dispatch. An inexpensive alternative, at Wong's Wok you'll find many WashU students and St. Louis area natives dining in and carrying out. We love to serve our customers our many delicious dishes!
