About Us

Located on Melville just south of the Delmar Loop, Wong's Wok is a staple in the area for students and locals alike. With a 4-star rating on Yelp and 92% rating on Urbanspoon, Wong's Wok is a popular, inexpensive Chinese restaurant. Those who enjoy authentic Chinese food, as well as Americanized alternatives, will appreciate Wong's Wok's tasty dishes. Come dine in or take out! We're also more than happy to deliver! Enjoy our delicious food in our restaurant or at home.
